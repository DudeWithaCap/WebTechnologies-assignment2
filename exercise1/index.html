Bauyrzhan Momyshuly

Kazakh Military Hero & Writer

Example of Great Leadership

Bauyrzhan Momyshuly is a famous Kazakh military officer, writer and national hero. He was born on December 24, 1910 in Zhambyl region. Soviet officer, participant of the Soviet-Japanese War and the Great Patriotic War. Known for the battle of the Volokolamsk Highway and the defense of Moscow, which were recorded in his books.
